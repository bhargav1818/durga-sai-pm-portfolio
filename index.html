🤖 Case Study 1 — Gemini AI Receipt Processing Pipeline

POC → Production · Germany Global Rollout · 10+ LLMs Benchmarked

  • Problem: Legacy OCR had ~15% failure rate on non-standard receipts, zero multilingual support, and couldn't scale to 2M+ receipts without spiralling manual review costs.
  • Approach: Structured UAT benchmarking 10+ LLMs across real-world multilingual receipt samples — selected Google Gemini Flash/Pro for accuracy, speed, and cost.
  • Camera UX: Real-time frame alignment, brightness scoring, blur detection — rejects bad captures at source, cutting rejection rates 40%.
  • Germany: First international rollout — Gemini auto-translates receipts, stores in user language preference. Architecture supports new countries in days.
  • Outcome: 98% accuracy · $800K+ annual savings · 2M+ receipts · 5+ languages · 10+ countries

🔍 Case Study 2 — Fraud Detection Model ($1.5M Prevented)

Root Cause Analysis · Bulk Email Fraud · 3-Tier Detection System

  • Problem: Coordinated bulk email fraud exploiting receipt submission reward system, generating fraudulent payouts at scale.
  • Approach: RCA — analysed email domain clustering, submission velocity anomalies, receipt duplication rates, IP geolocation clustering over 90-day window.
  • 3-Tier System: Low-risk → flagged for human review; Medium-risk → rate-limited with captcha; High-risk → auto-blocked with compliance audit trail.
  • Validation: Back-tested against 6 months of historical data; zero false-positive rate on legitimate high-volume trusted users.
  • Outcome: $1.5M eliminated · Zero major fraud incidents post-implementation · Full compliance audit trail.
$1.5M PreventedRCA Driven 3-Tier SystemZero False Positives
